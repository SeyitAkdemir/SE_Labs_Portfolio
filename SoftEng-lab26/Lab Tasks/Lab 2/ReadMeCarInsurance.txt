A Use Case Diagram illustrating the digital interface between a customer and an insurance provider.

Actors
Customer: The user seeking to manage their vehicle insurance policies.
InsuranceStaff:An actor to manage insurance payments and costumer inforrmations.
Damage Controller:Controls damage.

 Use Cases
Report Damage: Allows users to report accidents or damages.
Get Price:Provides a read-only view of the current coverage.
Buy Insurance: Handles the financial transactions for renewals or new plans.
